            We're Glad You're Here.

            Beautiful spaces. Thoughtful amenities. Memorable details. Set in the heart of Silicon Valley, steps from Santana Row and Westfield Valley Fair, Windsor Winchester offers modern design, personalized service, and a convenient neighborhood all in one.

            Go for a dip in the resort-style pool, lounge in a private cabana, or relax by the outdoor fireplace. Catch a movie in the screening lounge with cinema-style seating, work from home in cozy co-working spaces, or stay active in the fitness center with Echelon Reflect mirror and bikes. Hang outs in the resident kitchen and social lounge turn any ordinary night into lasting memories.

            Clean, modern lines, high ceilings, and bright, airy floor plans are just the start. Chef-inspired kitchens featuring Italian cabinetry with soft-close drawers, quartz or granite countertops, and full-height backsplashes make every meal feel special. Its hard not to admire our spacious bedrooms, spa-inspired bathrooms with frameless glass showers or soaking tubs, and high-end touches.

            Every detail is tailored to the way you live, work, and unwind.

            Community Amenities

            Location

            • Located in the heart of Silicon Valley, offering unmatched access to tech hubs, shopping, and entertainment
            • Steps from Santana Row and Westfield Valley Fair, featuring upscale retail, dining, and nightlife
            • Easy access to Downtown San Jose and major freeways including I-280 and I-880 for seamless commuting
            • Adjacent to the historic Winchester Mystery House, adding charm and cultural appeal to the neighborhood
            • Walkable to parks, cafes, and everyday conveniences, making errands and leisure effortless

            Lifestyle

            • Resort-style pool with private cabanas
            • Elevated green space with outdoor fireplace and cozy seating
            • Expansive social courtyards with fire pits, grills, and open-air dining
            • Screening lounge with HD projector and cinema-style seating
            • 24-hour fitness center with Echelon Reflect mirror & bikes
            • Resident social events
            • Pet-friendly community with dog run, grooming stations, and exclusive dog park
            • Smoke-free community

            Convenience

            • Flexible co-working spaces and private meeting rooms
            • Resident bike lounge and storage, repair stations, and bike wash
            • EV charging stations
            • LuxerOne package locker system with refrigerated storage
            • Onsite management
            • Onsite 24-hour emergency maintenance
            • Wifi access in common areas
            • Online payments and maintenance portal
